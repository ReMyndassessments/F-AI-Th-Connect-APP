The future is AI. Will our faith be a part of it?

Artificial Intelligence is rapidly transforming our world. It's reshaping industries, influencing our decisions, and becoming an integral part of the next generation's lives. As Christians, we have a vital responsibility to ensure that our values, principles, and faith are not left behind in this technological revolution.
