- Clear cursor visibility
- Smooth mouse movements

**Content to Record**:
1. App loading and main interface
2. AI chat conversation with biblical question
3. Bible verse lookup with multiple translations
4. Text-to-speech demonstration

## Post-Production Guidelines

### 1. Video Editing
- **Software**: Adobe Premiere Pro, Final Cut Pro, or DaVinci Resolve
- **Transitions**: Clean cuts with 0.5s fade transitions
- **Text Overlays**: Highlight key features with callout text
- **Zoom Effects**: Subtle zoom-ins for important details

### 2. Audio Production
- **Narration**: Professional, warm, and clear voice
- **Background Music**: Subtle Christian instrumental music
- **Audio Levels**: Narration at -12dB, music at -24dB
- **Sync**: Precise timing with visual demonstrations

### 3. Visual Enhancements
- **Callouts**: Highlight buttons and interface elements
- **Animations**: Smooth transitions between sections
- **Branding**: F-AI-TH-Connect logo placement
- **Quality**: Maintain 1080p resolution throughout
