Title: Walking in Faith

Main Scripture: Hebrews 11:1 - "Now faith is confidence in what we hope for and assurance about what we do not see."

Key Points:
1. Faith requires trust in God's promises
2. Faith is demonstrated through our actions
3. Faith grows through trials and challenges

Application:
- Pray daily for increased faith
- Study examples of faithful believers in Scripture
- Take practical steps of obedience even when unclear about outcomes

Discussion Questions:
1. What does it mean to have "confidence in what we hope for"?
2. How can we grow our faith during difficult times?
3. What practical steps can we take this week to demonstrate faith?
